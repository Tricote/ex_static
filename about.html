About AccessTrial

At AccessTrial, we're working to improve access to clinical trials for patients who could benefit from them. Our platform helps connect patients with relevant research studies, making it easier to navigate what can often be a complex and overwhelming process of finding and enrolling in potentially life-changing treatments.

We combine battle-tested technologies with modern innovations to tackle real healthcare challenges:

  • Elixir & Phoenix LiveView: Our backend is built on Elixir of course üòá
  • PostgreSQL: Our go-to database for reliable data storage and queries
  • Large Language Models (LLMs): AI-powered matching algorithms to connect patients with the most relevant trials
